Welcome to my Poetry scrapbook

Today is World Poetry Day so I thought I'd share a few of my poems. The Majority of these poems were written in 2018 when I was staying in Pisa. I will add new poems now and then. As my writing style is to create a quick flowing poem in one (mosty) and as I am an awful proof reader , the poems with constantly be edited and updated, some may become even more streamlined and others more bulky,however I will be keeping the raw unedited versions and may in the future upload them as a insight into my creative process. Please also check out my interesting links page which lists some poets I'm reading at the moment and some general sources for poetry.

Enjoy reading! Hayden
